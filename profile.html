Biomedical Engineering

Biomedical Engineering is the application of engineering principles and design concepts to medicine and biology. Biomedical engineering bridges the gap between engineering and medicine, seamlessly combining the design and problem-solving skills of engineering with medical and biological sciences in order to improve healthcare diagnosis, monitoring and therapy.

Biomedical Engineering education trains engineers who can analyse multidisciplinary problems from the engineering, biological and medical perspectives, in particular, the complications in working with living systems.

The Biomedical Engineering degree programme aims to produce engineers who can contribute back to society through innovation, entrepreneurship and leadership. Graduates will be equipped with a strong foundation suitable for a flexible range of careers across R&D, product design and manufacturing in the Biomedical engineering industries, Bio technology Industries, Health care sectors, Pharmaceutical Companies, Research Industries, Government agencies, Multispeciality Hospitals, Educational Institutions and Universities or for further education in India and Abroad.


Career Prospects

Biomedical engineering is a growing field around the world, and India is deeply committed to establishing this related industry in South India, with the launch of many Bio medical industries, R&D centers, Health care Industries and Multispeciality Hospitals.

Our graduates have gone on to successful careers in Sethu Institute of Technology, with some being recruited by biomedical start- up companies , MNCs, biotechnology start-up companies, and numerous central government and state government agencies. Some have chosen to foreign universities on academia and pursue their Masters and PhD degrees.
